We are HYPERFLIGHT

Our company manufactures drones that scan a designated city or area for various factors such as for identifying slum areas, water logged areas, pollution and waste lands across a city. Due to these factors, human life quality and the environment is severly affected.

In order to address these problems , when our drone identifies an adversely affected area due to the factors mentioned above, it reports the problem to the government and displays it status on our online portal, which also helps to make people aware about the problems. Status and the actions taken by the people and the government are updated daily on the portal. Moreover, a daily report of the city is given by our drone to encounter problems that can occur in the future due to the current conditions in the designated area.

Our Services

Why HYPERFLIGHT

Data-Driven Solutions

Our drones provide accurate and real-time data on critical urban issues such as slum areas, waterlogged zones, pollution hotspots, and waste lands. By leveraging this data, governments can make informed decisions and implement targeted interventions to improve the quality of life for the community

Safe Future

By generating a report on daily basis of the area designated to our drones, it help cities become more resilient to potential future challenges by giving insights about the challenges which can be faced in future. With the help of this city authorities can proactively address emerging issues, preventing them from escalating into major problems.

Public Awareness and Engagement

Our online portal serves as an essential tool for creating public awareness about urban challenges. By displaying the status of identified problem areas and actions taken by the government, citizens become active participants in driving positive change and holding their authorities accountable.

Sustainable Development

Through our drone technology, we contribute directly to SDG 11- Sustainable Cities and Communities. By providing data-driven insights and fostering collaboration between citizens and governments, we strive to create greener, cleaner, healthier ,and more livable cities for generations to come.

Our Services

Environmental Monitoring and Pollution Mapping

Our drones conduct regular surveys to identify pollution sources, waste lands, and other environmental issues that impact the city's ecosystem. The data collected helps in creating pollution maps and implementing targeted pollution control measures.

Slum Area Identification and Urban Planning

Our drones are equipped to identify slum areas and provide crucial insights for urban planners and governments. This information aids in formulating inclusive development strategies and improving living conditions for marginalized communities.

Flood and Waterlogging Assessment

By scanning areas prone to waterlogging, our drones help in predicting and mitigating potential flood risks. This enables city authorities to take proactive measures, such as improving drainage systems and flood control infrastructure.

Real-time Reporting and Government Collaboration

Our drones instantly report identified problem areas to relevant government departments through a streamlined communication system. This collaboration ensures timely action and a coordinated response to address urban challenges.

Online Portal for Public Awareness

We offer an interactive online portal that displays the status of identified issues and actions taken by both the government and citizens. This platform raises public awareness and fosters engagement in finding sustainable solutions.

Future Problem Prediction

Our drones generate daily reports on the city's conditions, helping predict future challenges that may arise due to current environmental factors. This foresight assists city planners in proactive decision-making and long-term urban development.

GIS Mapping and Data Analytics

We provide Geographic Information System (GIS) mapping services and data analytics to help city officials visualize and analyze the collected data effectively. This empowers evidence-based decision-making for sustainable urban development.

Capacity Building and Training

We offer training programs to local authorities and drone operators, enabling them to utilize the technology effectively, interpret data, and implement appropriate measures for urban improvement.

Continuous Support and Maintenance

Our company provides ongoing technical support, maintenance, and updates to ensure that the drone operations run smoothly and efficiently over time.
